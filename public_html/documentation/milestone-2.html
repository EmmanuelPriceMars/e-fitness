content strategy

The contents of this website are very simple. As simple a workout library for different age groups and skill levels. Beginner, intermediate, and advanced are the tree levels of workouts available throughout the website. each level of difficulty has its own subsection for a leg workout, upper body workout, abs, and full body. The workouts are then illustrated with .gif images and explained properly. the best feature of this website is the age group selector, that will allow the users to select the age group or category they belong to. only 3 age groups will be available kids -young adults -elderly. the user selects his age group, his level of difficulty, and is delivered and workout suited for their needs.
